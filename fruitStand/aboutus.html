We grew up in this area. Our homes was in the middle of our many orchards. Picking the fruit off the tree at perfect ripeness was a luxury we didn't relize until we were adults. We started our stand so others could experience fruit the way we did when we were young. We talked with all our neighbors set up our first fruit stand.

We grew quickly and many people kept wishing they didn't have to drive as far to get our fruit. This is when we decied to become mobile. We started with food truck and now we have two. You can find where our trucks are on our social media sites.
