Target Audience

Riverton Chamber of Commerce wants to meet the needs of business owners and those who want find local people to work with.

Personas

Frank is 35 years old. He has an associates degree. He's been makeing websites since he was 14 years old. Today he visits the site to let the chamber know that he has some issues with the city's rules about his location of buisness. He has no customers comming to his house so why do he neighbors need to know he has a buisness?

Citizen

Alice is 28 years old. She is a school teacher that works at Riverton Elementary. She wants to support local business. She is in need of a photographer and visit the Chamber's site to find one in her own city.
