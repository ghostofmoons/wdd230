Riverton Demographics

Population

Riverton is the 19th most populated city in Utah with 44,773 people.

Race & Ethnicity

The largest Riverton racial/ethnic groups are White (86.5%) followed by Hispanic (7.9%) and Asian (2.1%).

Median Income

In 2021, the median household income of Riverton households was $107,674.

Median Housing

Median owner-occupied house in 2021 was $397,000.

Median Age

Riverton is pretty young with an average age of 32.6 years.

Family and Living Arrangements

In 2021 the number of households in Riverton was 13,248 with and average of 3.37 persons per household.

Education

94.1% of Riverton's population has a highschool deploma or higher. Inclucding the 39.5% with a bachelor's degree or higher.

Government

Six member council

Riverton, Utah is a city of the 3rd class as defined by Utah State Code. Riverton City operates using a “six-member council" form of government, which means there are six elected officials that make up the governing body: a mayor and five councilmembers. [See: Utah Code § 10-3b-301.]
